Team USA is the name used for the United States Olympic and Paralympic teams. Athletes compete under the American flag in summer and winter games.
The next Summer Olympics are in Los Angeles in 2028. The next Winter Olympics are in 2026.
You can follow official Team USA channels, watch events, and support athletes through official partners and fan programs.
Team USA competes in all Olympic sports, including athletics, swimming, gymnastics, basketball, and many more summer and winter disciplines.
